Featured Song: Triple Baka

"Triple Baka" is a famous Vocaloid song featuring Hatsune Miku, Akita Neru, and Kasane Teto. The song became popular because of its energetic style, funny lyrics, and memorable animation.

Featured Characters

Hatsune Miku

A famous virtual singer created by Crypton Future Media. She is known for her turquoise twin tails and worldwide popularity.

Akita Neru

A fan-created character known for her yellow hair, cellphone, and energetic personality.

Kasane Teto

A popular UTAU voicebank character recognized by her pink hair and drill-shaped twin tails.

Why Triple Baka is Popular

Triple Baka continues to be loved by fans because of its unique characters, catchy music, and creative community. Fans continue to create artwork, remixes, animations, and new content inspired by the trio.
